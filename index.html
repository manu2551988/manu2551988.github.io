About Me

I'm a passionate and growth-driven Quality Assurance Engineer I with ~5 years of industry experience, currently contributing to high-quality app and SDK experiences on Amazon Appstore and Fire TV platforms.

Career Journey: Started as a QA Tester at TCS, progressed to Senior QA Engineer at Nagarro, and now thriving as QAE I at Amazon. My expertise spans automation frameworks, SDK testing, performance optimization, and cross-platform compatibility testing across mobile, web, and TV platforms.

Key Achievements: Reduced testing cycles by 60% through automation, implemented performance monitoring that improved app launch times by 40%, and established testing standards adopted across multiple teams. I've successfully delivered quality solutions for high-traffic applications serving millions of users.

Technical Expertise: I specialize in end-to-end automation, performance profiling (TTFF, TTFD, memory), API testing, and cross-platform mobile/web testing. My approach combines technical depth with customer-centric thinking to ensure exceptional user experiences.
